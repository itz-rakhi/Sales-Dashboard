# 📊 Sales Dashboard | Power BI

An interactive **Power BI Sales Dashboard** built to analyze retail business performance through dynamic KPIs, sales trends, profitability analysis, and interactive filters. This dashboard helps businesses monitor key metrics and make data-driven decisions.

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project provides an executive-level overview of sales performance using Power BI. It transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, allowing users to explore sales by region, category, payment mode, store type, and product.

The dashboard is designed for business managers and decision-makers to quickly identify trends, monitor KPIs, and evaluate overall business performance.

## 💡 Key Business Insights

- Generated **₹1.05 Billion** in Gross Sales.
- Achieved **₹882.09 Million** in Net Sales.
- Earned **₹174.46 Million** in Total Profit.
- Electronics is the highest profit-generating category.
- Sales reached their highest point during the middle of the year before gradually declining.
- One region contributed over **₹315 Million** in Net Sales.
- Boat Television generated the highest product profit.
