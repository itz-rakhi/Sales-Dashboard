Executive Summary Dashboard – Power BI
📌 Overview

The Executive Summary Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide a comprehensive overview of retail business performance. It enables users to monitor key business metrics, analyze sales trends, evaluate profitability, and compare regional and product performance through dynamic visualizations and filters.

🚀 Features
📈 Interactive KPI cards displaying overall business performance.
📅 Dynamic filtering by Category, Region, Month, and Year.
📊 Monthly sales trend analysis.
🏬 Store type performance comparison.
💳 Payment mode analysis.
🌍 Regional sales comparison.
🛍️ Category-wise profit analysis.
🏆 Top profitable product insights.

📈 Dashboard Visualizations
🔹 Net Sales by Store Type

A donut chart showing the contribution of different store types to total net sales.

🔹 Net Sales by Month

A line chart displaying monthly sales performance, helping identify trends and seasonal patterns.

🔹 Total Profit by Category

A horizontal bar chart comparing profit across product categories, with Electronics being the highest contributor.

🔹 Net Sales by Payment Mode

A pie chart illustrating customer payment preferences.

🔹 Net Sales by Region

A bar chart comparing regional sales performance and identifying the best-performing regions.
